Golf Claw

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Plato Labs ("Plato Labs," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Golf Claw (the "App"). Golf Claw is an iOS app designed to help golfers review swing videos with AI-assisted analysis.

Information We Collect

Golf Claw may collect information you provide or generate while using the App, such as swing videos, images, analysis results, practice notes, preferences, and other golf-related activity you choose to save.

If you upload or record a swing video, the App may process visual information from that video to identify movement patterns, swing positions, timing, and other golf instruction signals. Depending on the features available in the App, processing may occur on your device or through trusted service providers.

If Golf Claw offers optional location-based features, the App may request access to your device location to support golf-related functionality. You can control location permissions in iOS Settings.

We may also receive technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports, diagnostics, and general usage information to help maintain and improve the App.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide Golf Claw's features, save your app preferences, analyze swing videos, generate AI-assisted feedback, improve performance, troubleshoot issues, develop new features, protect the App, and respond to support requests.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate, analyze, or support the App, including providers that help process swing videos or provide AI-assisted analysis. We may also share information when required to comply with law, enforce our rights, protect users, or complete a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

Third-Party Services

Golf Claw may rely on Apple services and other trusted service providers for app distribution, diagnostics, analytics, hosting, media storage, support, or AI-assisted video analysis. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the App, improve our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of personal information by contacting us.

Your Choices

You can manage App permissions, including location access and notifications, through iOS Settings. You may also contact us to ask about accessing, correcting, or deleting personal information associated with Golf Claw.

Children's Privacy

Golf Claw is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Golf Claw's privacy practices, contact Plato Labs at hello@platolabs.app.
